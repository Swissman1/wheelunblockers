Targeting Predatory Enforcement

No more unjust tows!

Paid parking is a ubiquitous part of the Charlotte experience. Increasingly however, property owners are utilizing less than reasonable, and outright illegal fly-by-night tow operators.

The current legal landscape for protecting citizens is shaky, with no hard caps and little proactive enforcement of existing statute. Businesses should be allowed to protect their lots, but only through accountable, ethical operators.

Project Goals

  • Expose: Identify below board operators and shame businesses who use them.Map Search
  • Highlight:Identify businesses that follow the rules.Map Search
  • Educate: Inform citizens of their rights with our Field Guide.
  • Assist: Help victims recover funds from bad-faith operators.
  • Advocate: Push for legislation to rein in predatory booting.

This project thrives on crowd-sourced information.
