About us / get involved. This project was started by a citizen, who suffered a 275 boot fee by a non-registered ghost operator doing parking enforcement on a abandoned property. Doing the background to fight their extortion, it was revealed how widespread The rogue booting / towing problem is in Charlotte.  This project is not against paid parking, nor towing and booting. It seeks to stop bad faith companies, and promote fair companies.This project is civilian-led, and crowdsourced. If you would like to join the fight there are a myriad of ways.  Help compile parking signs:  Photograph "no parking", "towing enforced" signs with their location, and email them to wheelunblockers Spread the word: helping people understand they have recourse if they've been unjustly charged, Is a huge thing. These companies rely on the expectation that you will just pay it and move on.
